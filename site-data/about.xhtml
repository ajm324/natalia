About Natalia Lafourcade

Natalia Lafourcade is a Mexican singer-songwriter who specializes in pop-rock and folk. She made her musical debut with her self-titled album in 2002 and as time has progressed, she has become a significant figure in Latin America and the world as a whole. She pulls on experiences from her past and elements of national imagery and patriotic identity to create timeless music that resonates with the latinx community and many outside of it.
